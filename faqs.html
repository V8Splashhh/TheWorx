Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about The Worx

What is The Worx?

The Worx is a coworking space in Port of Spain that fosters innovation and collaboration among professionals. Our space brings people together, is rooted in trust and collaboration, and allows people to interact successfully and grow together.

What are your operating hours?

We are open from 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM every day. 24/7 access for members who require it will be available in the future

Where are you located?

74 Long Circular Road in the CIBC Building. You must check in with secuirty for parking spaces and guidance through the building.

Can I tour the space before joining?

Absolutely! We encourage prospective members to visit and experience The Worx firsthand. Book a tour through our contact form or call us to schedule a visit at your convenience.

What membership options are available?

We offer Day Passes, Hot Desk memberships, Dedicated Desk memberships, and Private Office spaces. Each tier includes different amenities and access levels. Visit our Services page for detailed pricing and features.

Can I change my membership plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your membership at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Contact our member services team to make changes to your plan.

Is there a contract or commitment period?

Our memberships are month-to-month with no long-term contracts required. We also offer annual plans with a 15% discount for those who prefer to commit for a year.

What's your cancellation policy?

You can cancel your membership anytime with 30 days notice. Simply notify us through your member portal or email, and your membership will end at the next billing cycle.

What amenities are included?

All memberships include high-speed Wi-Fi, unlimited coffee and tea, printing services (limits vary by plan), access to common areas, kitchen facilities, and phone booths. Higher-tier plans include additional perks like meeting room credits and 24/7 access.

Is parking available?

Yes, we have parking available. Day pass holders can use hourly parking at discounted rates. Monthly members can add a reserved parking spot to their membership for an additional fee.

Do you have private phone booths?

Yes, we have several soundproof phone booths available for private calls and video conferences. These are available on a first-come, first-served basis for all members.

Can I receive mail at The Worx?

Dedicated desk and private office members can receive mail at our address. We also offer virtual office services for those who need a business address without physical workspace.

How do I book a meeting room?

Meeting rooms can be booked through your member portal or on our Services page. Select your preferred room, date, and time. Bookings can be made up to 30 days in advance.

What's the cancellation policy for room bookings?

Meeting room bookings can be cancelled up to 24 hours before the scheduled time for a full refund or credit. Cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance will be charged 50% of the booking fee.

Can non-members book meeting rooms?

Yes, meeting rooms are available to non-members at standard rates. Members receive discounted rates and some plans include free meeting room hours each month.

Do you offer catering for meetings?

Yes, we partner with local cafes and restaurants to provide catering options for your meetings. Options range from coffee and pastries to full lunch spreads. Order at least 48 hours in advance.
